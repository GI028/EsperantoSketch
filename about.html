About Us

Address

Reveal in the Map

Esperanto private school is in the center of the village called Milenaka, capital of the Rural Commune of Milenaka, district of Toliara II, and is located 63 km in the North of Toliara city, along the National Road No 9 (RN9 / Toliara-Morombe), near to the Mikea Forest.

Vision

Esperanto private school will train responsible citizens, respectful of others, engaged in their community, proud of their culture, and students that will develop their skills globally throughout their lives.

History

Esperanto private school was founded in September 2007 by Mr Ralaivao Rakotonirina Emilson, teacher at the junior public high school of Milenaka, Toliara II, Madagascar.

Mission

  • Produce high quality achievements in education.
  • Realizing an effective and efficient teaching and learning process.
  • Realizing the development of friendly environment in learning.
  • Creating a clean and green school.
  • Creating a drug-free school.
  • Educate and cultivate students to make them dignified, reasonable, patriotic, responsible, able to live according to their principles, and above all model students within the community.
  • Take care of children from low-income families and facilitate the payment of school fees.
